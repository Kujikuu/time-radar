Quick help

How does TimeRadar work?

TimeRadar is a local-first focus timer: create a task, start a focus block, and watch your daily radar signal. Tasks, sessions, and settings stay on your device.

Start quickly

Use the Start Focus button from the home screen.

Organize tasks

Add work, study, or personal focus tasks.

Review progress

Use Stats to review focus time and distribution.

Supporter Pack

Completely optional

Core focus features stay free. The $1.99 Supporter Pack supports development and unlocks a warmer visual theme.

Frequently asked questions

Does the app need an account?

No. TimeRadar has no login or user account.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. The app does not send your tasks or stats to an external server.

How do I restore the Supporter Pack?

Open Stats or Settings and choose Restore Purchase.
