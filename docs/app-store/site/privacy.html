Privacy Policy

TimeRadar is designed as a local-first focus timer. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data from the app.

Data stored on your device

TimeRadar stores your focus tasks, timer settings, local session history, language preference, notification preferences, and Supporter Pack entitlement status on your device.

Accounts

TimeRadar does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, or profile.

Analytics and tracking

TimeRadar does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, TimeRadar schedules local timer alerts on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally.

In-app purchases

The optional Supporter Pack is processed by Apple In-App Purchase. TimeRadar stores only a local entitlement state so the app can remember whether the pack is active.

Contact and changes

For privacy or support questions, contact radar@afifistudio.com. If this policy changes, the updated policy will be published at this URL.
