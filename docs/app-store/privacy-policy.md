# TimeRadar Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

TimeRadar is designed as a local-first focus timer. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data from the app.

## Data Stored On Your Device

TimeRadar stores your focus tasks, timer settings, local session history, language preference, notification preferences, and Supporter Pack entitlement status on your device. This information is used only to make the app work and is not sent to our servers.

## Accounts

TimeRadar does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, or profile.

## Analytics And Tracking

TimeRadar does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. We do not track you across apps or websites.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, TimeRadar schedules local timer alerts on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally.

## In-App Purchases

TimeRadar offers an optional one-time Supporter Pack through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes the payment. TimeRadar stores only a local entitlement state so the app can remember whether the Supporter Pack is active.
